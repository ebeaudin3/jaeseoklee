FIELDWORK

The contrast between computer-based data analysis and outdoor fieldwork is another charm of seismology. I have participated in a number of research projects deploying broadband seismometers and geophone arrays at various sites in the Korean Peninsula.

I am familiar with the broadband seismometer Trillium Compact, the digital recorders Centaur and Taurus, and the geophone SmartSolo IGU-16HR 3C. The acquired data are currently being used by the lab members for their research.
